MyScript Music

  • Technology
  • Music

    • MyScript Music enables the recognition of music scores created with digital ink. It is ideal for building applications for teaching music in the classroom or for empowering the mobile musician.

    Features

    Music elements

    MyScript Music can recognize more than 100 music elements and combinations of music elements.

    Single staff music scores

    MyScript Music can recognize single staff music score - multiple staff line music is not supported yet (e.g. piano or orchestra scores).

    Staff lines

    There is no need to handwrite staff lines with MyScript Music. Staff position simply has to be specified through an appropriated API function.

    Edition gestures

    MyScript Music supports edition gestures such as scratch-outs.
