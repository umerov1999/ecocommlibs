MyScript Math

  • Technology
  • Math

    • MyScript Math enables the recognition of handwritten equations, mathematical and scientific formulas or expressions, created with digital ink. It is ideal for math teaching application design.

    Features

    Symbols and rules

    MyScript Math can recognize 14 mathematical rules and more than 200 mathematical symbols and characters.

    Recursiveness in expressions

    MyScript Math can recognize equation elements with almost limitless levels of recursiveness (for example, square roots with subscript).

    Multiline equations

    Beyond recognizing single equations, MyScript Math can recognize multiline equations as well as systems of equations.

    Columnar operations

    MyScript Math can recognize basic columnar operations: additions, subtractions, multiplications and divisions.

    Matrices

    MyScript Math can recognize matrices.

    Extending recognition power

    It is possible to make a custom grammar resource. This allows specializing the recognition to particular cases of use (integral calculus, vector calculus, finite element calculus and so on).

    Editing gestures

    MyScript Math supports edition gestures such as scratch-outs.

    There are many more possibilities, such as:

    • some fence rules where left and right symbols do not match. For instance [a, b),
    • vectors,
    • arcs,
    • equations for trigonometry,
    • exponentiality in the variables. For example, variables of a fraction can also have subscript or superscript, partial fractions.
    • geometry and so on.
